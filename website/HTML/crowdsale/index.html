Crowdsale

The Ethbet Crowdsale is set to begin on September 17 2017 8PM UTC, lasting for only four weeks

Crowdsale Information

The Ethbet Crowdsale is scheduled to begin on September 17, 2017, 8PM UTC. It will last for exactly four weeks after this period.
Crowdsale not yet active
The ticker symbol for Ethbet is EBET. The total amount of possible tokens is set to 10,000,000. Of these tokens, 80% are available during the crowdsale (5% bounty/community, 5% partners, 10% Ethbet). The EBET allocated to Ethbet will be locked for 6 months by smart contract, ensuring it is in our best interest to make Ethbet succeed. In addition to this, all unsold tokens will be burnt after the crowdsale to prevent token dilution. There is currently 1 open slot for an additional partner in the Ethbet crowdsale, email team@ethbet.io if you would like to apply.
With a maximum possible sale of 8,000,000 tokens at the best rate of 1,500 EBET per ETH, the Ethbet crowdsale will reach its hard cap and will close to new investors after a total of 5333 ETH have been contributed to it. This low hard cap ensures that we will not be significantly over-funded and that our token holders will each be able to own a large proportion of EBET.
The base exchange rate is 1,000 EBET per ETH. The early investor bonuses remaining are listed in the following table.

The Ethbet token is fully ERC20-compliant, ensuring that it will already be supported with a large amount of wallets and exchanges the day of the crowdsale. Additional tokens will not be created after the crowdsale, and unsold tokens will be deleted to prevent token dilution.

The Ethbet token (EBET)

Notice: The Ethbet token has been improved and is now used to power the Ethbet platform instead of providing dividends. For information on this change and why it is beneficial, please read https://medium.com/@ethbet/changes-to-the-ethbet-token-model-e4eef4923b91.

Ethbet token holders possess the currency that powers the Ethbet protocol.

As Ethbet gains more adoption, there are several factors which can contribute to the value of Ethbet tokens, including increased consumer demand, increased token utility, increased token value due to network effects, and increased value due to its deflationary nature and buy-back demand from Ethbet smart contracts.

Ethbet token holders are also able to vote on proposals to improve Ethbet. Token holders can vote on values such as what Ethbet's cut of its optional player-defined house edge should be or what future projects Ethbet should pursue, allowing them to steer the platform in the direction that is most profitable.

For more inforamtion about how Ethbet and its token functions, please read the Ethbet whitepaper.

How to take part in the Ethbet Crowdsale

1. Wait until September 17th, 8PM UTC. This will occur in .
2. Use a wallet that supports tokens, such as MyEtherWallet, Metamask, Parity, or Mist.
3. Move Ethereum into your wallet. Ethereum is the only currency that is accepted in the Ethbet crowdsale. If you do not have Ethereum, you can buy it from websites such as Coinbase, or use a service such as Shapeshift to exchange other cryptocurrencies for Ethereum. Be sure to securely store and back up your private key.
4. Send the desired amount of ETH to the crowdsale wallet address below (not yet shown). Set your 'gas' to 200,000 for a smooth transaction. You will receive 1,000 EBET plus an early investor bonus per ETH that you send. The current investor bonus will be displayed.
More detailed information will be posted prior to the date of the crowdsale. If you have any questions about the crowdsale, don't hesitate to send us an email.
Do not send ETH from an exchange wallet such as Coinbase, Poloniex, and many others. These wallets do not support ERC20 tokens, including Ethbet. Your tokens will be lost if you use a wallet that does not support tokens.
Do not participate in the crowdsale if you have not read or do not understand the risks involved with platforms such as Ethereum and Ethbet.
Do not attempt to participate in the crowdsale if you are a resident of the United States. Residents in the United States are not allowed to participate in the Ethbet crowdsale due to the complex and costly requirements that the SEC would require.
